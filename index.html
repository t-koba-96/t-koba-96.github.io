About me

I’m a first year Master’s student who belongs to Professor Aoki’s laboratory at Keio University. Though I belong to the Department of Electronic Engineering, I am majoring in Computer Vision topics. More specifically, my main research topic is human action recognition/ segmentation using fine-grained videos.

I used to live in Singapore for 4years in my childhood, so I have no hesitation speaking English. As my hobby, I like playing baseball, going to the gym for training, and traveling foreign countrys. The background pictures on the top page are some of the places I have travelled.
I am always looking forward for the opportunity to experience new things & brush up my skills of Computer Vision and Programming.
